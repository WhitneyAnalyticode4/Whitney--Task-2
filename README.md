## Revenue Trend Analysis

Yearly sales performance showed a gradual decline:

| Year               |     Revenue |
| :----------------- | ----------: |
| 2023               | ₦552,441.24 |
| 2024               | ₦480,215.87 |
| 2025 (YTD Jan–Jun) | ₦231,882.85 |

### Key Observation

* 2023 recorded the highest revenue.
* Revenue declined by approximately **13% in 2024** compared to 2023.
* 2025 year-to-date revenue remains lower and requires further investigation to determine whether the decline is due to incomplete data, changing customer behavior, or reduced sales performance.

---

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Product Performance

* Laptop recorded the highest order count with **173 transactions**.
* Desk and other high-performing products contributed significantly to overall sales.
* Product demand insights can guide inventory planning and promotional campaigns.

### 2. Payment Behavior

* Online payment was the most frequently used payment method with **258 transactions**.
* Credit Card and Debit Card were also popular payment options, showing a preference for digital payments.

### 3. Order Fulfillment Analysis

* Shipped orders recorded the highest order status with **241 orders**.
* Cancelled, Returned, and Pending orders indicate areas where fulfillment efficiency can be improved.

### 4. Marketing Channel Performance

* Email and Google were the strongest referral sources driving customer orders.
* Underperforming channels should be reviewed to improve marketing return on investment.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts around high-performing products to maximize sales opportunities.
* Implement targeted campaigns for lower-performing products to improve demand.
* Optimize fulfillment processes to reduce cancelled, returned, and pending orders.
* Allocate more resources to effective referral channels such as Email and Google.
* Perform deeper analysis into the declining revenue trend to identify the root cause.
